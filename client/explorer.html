What is Collect?

For explorers who find a grid pattern that feels meaningful, you can collect that pattern as an NFT and become its Discoverer. During the process, you may include an inscription that will be permanently displayed alongside the work. Each pattern can have only one Discoverer.

A Note from Norun

You are always welcome to freely explore past patterns and participate in The Absurd Work project. The discoverer feature lets collectors support the project as patrons. I truly appreciate everyone who helps keep The Absurd Work running.
