What is Discover?

For explorers who find a grid pattern that feels meaningful, you have the opportunity to mint that pattern as an NFT and become its Discoverer. During the minting process, you may include an inscription that will be permanently displayed alongside the work. Each pattern can have only one Discoverer.

A Note from Norun

You are always welcome to freely explore past patterns and participate in The Absurd Work project. I added the minting feature as a way for collectors to become patrons and help me keep this project going. I really appreciate all the support that helps this project continue.

The Discovery & NFT Process

  1. Once a message is inscribed during the minting process, it cannot be edited or changed
  2. This NFT collection includes a 5% royalty on all secondary sales. These proceeds go directly to the original creator, Norun, to support ongoing work.
